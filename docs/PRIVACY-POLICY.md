# LuminaDeck Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: April 2026
**Last Updated**: April 2026

## Overview

LuminaDeck ("the App") is a macro deck application that allows you to control your Windows PC from your iPhone over your local WiFi network. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect analytics or usage telemetry
- We do not track your location
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not use advertising SDKs
- We do not record keystrokes or button presses
- We do not access your contacts, messages, or other personal data

### Data Stored Locally On Your Device
The following data is stored only on your iPhone and your PC, never transmitted to our servers:

- **Button layouts and profiles**: Your custom button configurations are saved to your device's local storage
- **Connection history**: IP addresses of paired PCs are stored in your device's secure keychain
- **Settings preferences**: Theme, haptic feedback, and grid layout preferences
- **TLS certificates**: Self-signed certificates for secure local communication

### Data We May Collect (Optional)
If you choose to create an account for Pro license management:

- **Email address**: Used only for account authentication
- **Purchase receipt**: Apple In-App Purchase transaction ID, validated for license entitlement

This data is stored in our Supabase backend and is not shared with any third parties.

## Network Communication

- All communication between your iPhone and PC occurs over your **local WiFi network only**
- All connections are encrypted with **TLS 1.3**
- No data is routed through our servers or any cloud relay
- mDNS discovery is used only during device pairing and is stopped once pairing completes

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple In-App Purchase**: Used for Pro upgrade processing. Subject to Apple's privacy policy.
- **RevenueCat**: Used for purchase receipt validation. RevenueCat processes only your anonymous App Store transaction data. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.
- **Supabase**: Used for optional account management. See Supabase's privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy.

## Children's Privacy

LuminaDeck does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

## Data Retention

- Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the App
- Account data (if created) is retained until you request deletion
- Purchase history is maintained by Apple per their retention policies

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the App (visible in Settings)
- **Delete**: Remove all local data by uninstalling the App
- **Account deletion**: Request deletion of any account data by contacting us
- **Export**: Export your button profiles as JSON files

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website and reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
